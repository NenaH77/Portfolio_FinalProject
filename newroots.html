NewRoots UX Case Study

A concept platform designed to help users access resources and guidance with clarity.

Project Type

UX Coursework

My Role

UX Designer & Project Lead

Focus Areas

Research, Accessibility, Wireframes, UI Design

Overview

NewRoots is a concept platform designed to support immigrants seeking legal residency by providing access to essential resources, guidance, and support services in one clear, user-friendly experience.

The Problem

Many users face challenges navigating complex legal systems, accessing reliable resources, understanding legal language, and overcoming language or accessibility barriers.

The Goal

The goal was to create a clear, accessible, and user-friendly platform that simplifies the process and helps users find the resources they need with confidence.

My Process

01

Research

I reviewed user needs, legal barriers, and accessibility challenges to better understand where users were experiencing confusion or friction.

02

Wireframes

I explored layout ideas, user flows, and screen structure before moving into polished interface designs.

03

Testing & Refinement

I used usability insights to improve clarity, accessibility, and the overall user journey, ensuring information was easy to understand and navigate.

Key Features

Real-time product availability Interactive store maps Personalized recommendations Simplified navigation

Design Takeaway

This project strengthened my understanding of empathy-driven design. I focused on reducing confusion, supporting accessibility, and creating a platform that could help users feel more informed, supported, and safe while navigating a difficult process.
